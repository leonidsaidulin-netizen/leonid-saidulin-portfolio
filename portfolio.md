## Research method

I use deep interviews to understand the customer's business process and form hypotheses.

1. Recruit contrasting cohorts: loyal customers, churned customers, non-buyers, competitor users, and industry specialists.
2. Write five or six hidden hypotheses in advance without exposing them through leading questions.
3. Ask the respondent to reconstruct the latest real case: who started it, where manual work appeared, what cost money, and who made the decision.
4. Check statements against behavior, CRM, support, SQL, and willingness to pay. Keep contradictory cases visible.
5. Use AI for transcription and clustering, replay key source fragments, and move only repeated, sourced signals into product strategy.
